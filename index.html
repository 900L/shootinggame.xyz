Tetris is the legendary puzzle game that has captivated players worldwide since 1984. Created by Russian game designer Alexey Pajitnov, this timeless classic challenges players to arrange falling geometric shapes called "Tetrominoes" to create complete horizontal lines.

The game's simple yet addictive gameplay has made it one of the most recognizable and beloved video games of all time. As you progress through levels, the pieces fall faster, testing your quick thinking and spatial reasoning skills.

üéÆ How to Play

The objective is simple: arrange the falling Tetromino pieces to create complete horizontal lines. When a line is completed, it disappears, and you earn points. The game ends when the pieces stack up to the top of the playing field.

Scoring: Clear single lines for basic points, but try to clear multiple lines simultaneously for bonus points. A "Tetris" (clearing four lines at once) gives you the highest score multiplier!
